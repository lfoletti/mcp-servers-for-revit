Impact-XL — blast-radius of a structural deletion (multi-hop dependency).

The current Revit project has levels, walls hosted on those levels, and
windows hosted on those walls. Using ONLY your project-memory tools — do
NOT click through the model, do NOT inspect the .rvt by hand.

For the level named "Niveau 0", compute its full structural blast radius:
everything that would be orphaned or destroyed if that level were deleted —
- every wall on that level,
- every window transitively hosted on any of those walls.

Report:
  1. The number of walls on Niveau 0.
  2. The number of windows transitively hosted on those walls.
  3. The total blast radius (walls + windows).
  4. A breakdown by hop distance from the level (hop 1 = walls, hop 2 =
     windows), with the count at each hop.

Do not modify anything. Walk the level -> walls -> windows dependency chain
with your tools in as few calls as possible, rather than re-querying Revit
element by element.
